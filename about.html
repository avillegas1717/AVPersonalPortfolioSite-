About Me

Hi, I’m Alicia Villegas — a front-end developer passionate about accessibility, clean code, and user-focused design. My journey into tech is a bit unconventional, but each chapter — from leadership to professional photography — sharpened my problem-solving, communication, and creativity, all skills I bring into any team.

I transitioned into tech by completing a full stack coding bootcamp, gaining hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more. While the program gave me a solid full stack foundation, I quickly gravitated toward front-end development, where design, accessibility, and user experience intersect.

Following bootcamp, I completed an IT help desk apprenticeship that combined certification preparation with 6 months of on-the-job training. During this time, I earned certifications including Microsoft 365 & Azure fundamentals as well as CompTIA A+. I also studied for Microsoft Endpoint Administrator and CompTIA Network+. The program wrapped up when I relocated, but the hands-on experience deepened my understanding of how systems work and how users interact with technology in the real world.

Today, my focus is on front-end development and making the web more accessible. I also teach with KCWiT’s (Kansas City Women in Technology) Coding & Cocktails program, where I’ve helped improve presentation slide accessibility while mentoring new learners. Teaching keeps my skills sharp and reinforces my commitment to writing thoughtful, inclusive code.

Accessibility is more than just a technical requirement for me — it’s a passion. I believe everyone deserves equal access to information and experiences on the web, regardless of ability. Creating inclusive designs that remove barriers and welcome all users drives me to write clean & thoughtful code.

In my free time you’ll usually find me exploring NW Arkansas with my dog Gizmo (parks, trails & pup-friendly coffee shops), painting, collecting vinyl, or catching live concerts. These hobbies keep me grounded, inspired, and remind me to bring creativity & rhythm into everything I build.

I'm a quick learner, clear communicator, and eager to tackle new challenges. I’m excited to join a team where I can grow as a front-end developer and help build web experiences that truly serve everyone.
